About Me

Hi, I'm Steven Shyaka, a passionate Software Engineer with a focus on Frontend Development, Machine Learning, and Mobile App Development. With years of hands-on experience, I specialize in using modern technologies to create meaningful solutions.

I started my journey in tech by diving into Frontend Development, mastering tools like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React and Vue.js. Over time, my curiosity led me to explore the power of Machine Learning.

I also enjoy developing mobile applications using Flutter, allowing me to build cross-platform apps that offer high performance and great user experience. My work spans several projects, from creating the Predikta event prediction platform, to FarmEd, a mobile app for educating farmers.

What drives me is my desire to leverage technology to address real-world challenges. My goal is to make a positive impact through innovation.

As a lifelong learner, I'm always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow, whether through furthering my technical skills or collaborating with teams that share my vision of using technology to improve lives.
